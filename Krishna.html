Once Upon a Time in Kadambapur

In the tranquil village of Kadambapur, nestled in the heart of Telangana, a bright star was born on August 4, 1997. Kommu Krishnaveni, affectionately known as Krishna, arrived with a gentle spirit and an insatiable curiosity that would shape her life's journey.

From her earliest days, Krishna stood apart — a seeker of knowledge and wonder. Surrounded by the loving guidance of her parents and the protective care of her two elder brothers, she blossomed into a natural leader, fueled by a thirst for learning and discovery.

  • Father: Shankaraiah
  • Mother: Kanthamma
  • Brothers: Venkatesh and Jagadeesh

Her academic path led her to earn a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Guru Nanak Institutions Technical Campus in Hyderabad. Yet, her college years were more than just studies; they were a time of self-discovery, resilience, and growth.

Beyond academics, Krishna’s adventurous heart found joy in exploring the world through her camera lens, capturing fleeting moments and turning them into timeless memories. Known as Kitty among friends, her artistic soul shines brightly in every frame.

Rooted deeply in her faith, Krishna is also known as Mary within her church community — a symbol of her inner peace and spiritual devotion. This beautiful balance of free spirit and steadfast values defines the essence of who she is.

At the core of her story is family — the unwavering support of her father’s wisdom, her mother’s boundless love, and the camaraderie of her brothers. Together, they have been her strength and inspiration.

Today, as we celebrate Krishna, we honor her laughter, her quiet strength, and the dreams that light her path. This is just the beginning of an incredible journey filled with endless possibilities.
